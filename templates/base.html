About Mango Disease Detection

This advanced AI system helps detect diseases in mango leaves using state-of-the-art deep learning models.

Detectable Diseases:
  • Anthracnose - Fungal disease causing dark spots
  • Bacterial Canker - Bacterial infection affecting stems
  • Cutting Weevil - Insect damage to leaves
  • Die Back - Progressive death of branches
  • Gall Midge - Insect-induced galls
  • Healthy - No disease detected
  • Powdery Mildew - White fungal growth
  • Sooty Mould - Black fungal coating
AI Features:
  • Multiple deep learning models (ResNet, EfficientNet, Vision Transformer)
  • Comprehensive explainability methods (GradCAM, LIME, Integrated Gradients)
  • Model comparison and ensemble predictions
  • Batch processing for multiple images
  • Real-time analysis with confidence scores
